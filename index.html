AI Background Remover (Demo)

This demo previews your image on a checkerboard to simulate transparency. Real background removal can be added by connecting an AI/image API.

Face Upscaler (Visual Demo)

This is a visual demo that applies gentle filters to simulate a sharper look. A real face upscaler can later be powered by dedicated image models.

Meme Generator (Canvas Demo)

Upload a picture, add top and bottom text and download a meme-style image.

Instagram Post Generator (Demo)

Type a topic and tone. The demo generates structured captions using simple rules. You can later connect this to an AI text API.

PDF & Text Summarizer (Demo)

Paste text and choose the level of compression. The demo uses simple slicing logic. A real AI summarizer can be plugged later.

AI Resume Builder (Demo)

Hashtag Generator (Demo)

Grammar Helper (Light Demo)

This helper applies simple cleanups: extra spaces, basic capitalization and final punctuation. It is not a full grammar checker, but a lightweight pre-clean step.
